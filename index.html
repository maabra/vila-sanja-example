Welcome to Vila Sanja

Vila Sanja is a family villa in Premantura, just 300 m from the village center and close to the sea. We offer a duplex apartment for 6, an apartment for 4, and a cozy room for 2 – all with private balconies, air conditioning, and free WiFi.

The villa is surrounded by greenery with a peaceful courtyard and private parking. Enjoy the Mediterranean atmosphere and the best Istria has to offer!
