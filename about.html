About Vila Sanja

Hosts: Sanja Volner & Matej Abramović

Vila Sanja is in a very good location, very close to the center and not far from the sea. The entrance to Cape Kamenjak is also nearby. We have a duplex apartment for 6 people, an apartment for 4 people, and a room with a bathroom and balcony. We are dedicated to making guests feel comfortable and are always available.

The entire villa is surrounded by greenery, and parking spaces are available in the villa's courtyard. We try to keep our prices reasonable. Peaceful surroundings – you can sleep well here, and everything you need is nearby. Welcome!

Staff speaks: German, English
