House Rules

House Rules & Information

Check-in & Check-out Times

Check-in: 15:00 - 21:00

Check-out: 06:00 - 10:00

A mandatory checkout inspection with property management is required before departure. Please coordinate your checkout time in advance.
Guests must present a valid photo ID upon check-in. Please inform Vila Sanja in advance of your expected arrival time.

Children & Beds

Children of all ages are welcome.

To see correct prices and occupancy information, please add the number and ages of children in your group to your search.

Crib and extra bed policy

  • Ages 0-5: Baby cot available upon request (free of charge)
  • No extra beds available
  • All cribs are subject to availability

Age Restriction

Minimum check-in age is 18 years.

Pets

Pets are allowed. Additional charges may apply - please contact us in advance.

Payment Information

Payment is arranged directly with your hosts. Please ensure you have cash available for any additional charges upon arrival.

Quiet Hours & Parties

Quiet hours: 22:00 - 09:00

Parties and events are not permitted.

Health & Safety

  • Enhanced cleaning and safety measures are in place
  • Physical distancing measures apply
  • Please ensure your booking complies with local guidelines
  • This property cannot be used for quarantine purposes

Frequently Asked Questions

What accommodation types are available at Vila Sanja?
Vila Sanja offers three options: Duplex Apartment (up to 4 + 2 people), One-Bedroom Apartment (up to 2 + 2 people), Double Room with Balcony (2 people).
How far is Vila Sanja from Premantura center?
Vila Sanja is located approximately 200-300 meters from the Premantura village center.
How far is the nearest beach?
The nearest beach is approximately 600-900 meters from Vila Sanja. Cape Kamenjak Nature Park entrance is also nearby.
What are the prices at Vila Sanja?
Prices vary depending on your stay dates, accommodation type, and group size. Contact us directly for current availability and prices.
Is parking available?
Yes, free private parking is available on site in the villa's courtyard.
